Manufacturing Intelligence Services

Face‑Level Traceability

Every detected issue is pinned to a specific CAD face with an exact measurement – no black boxes, no magic.

13 Manufacturing Detectors

Thin walls, holes, small radii, deep pockets, undercuts, draft, ribs/bosses, edges, hole‑edge proximity, AM support, setup feasibility, and tolerance sensitivity – all covered.

5 Process Modes

Machining, Molding, Casting, Sheet Metal, and Additive. Each mode applies custom thresholds and physics heuristics for accurate, process‑specific feedback.

📊

Severity‑Ranked Reports & Interactive Dashboard

Critical, High, Medium, Low – grouped issues, Top‑10 risks, executive summary. The interactive HTML report includes a readiness gauge, severity bars, and a design‑change simulator.

🧠

Adaptive Shop‑Brain

Override thresholds and mark issues as accepted. TIXAN remembers your choices and automatically softens future severities. Your shop’s knowledge stays local and private.

🔐

100% Offline & Private

Your CAD data never leaves your machine. No cloud, no internet required. Every analysis runs entirely on‑device, preserving your intellectual property.

🔬

Physics‑Based Heuristics

Recommendations are calculated from first‑principle formulas – cantilever deflection, tool reach, breakout risk – not from statistical models or AI. Pure, verifiable engineering.

📈

Manufacturing Maturity Score

A 1‑10 rating that instantly communicates how close your part is to production readiness. Combines issue severity, count, and process thresholds into one trusted number.

🖥️

3D Heatmap & Inspection Tools

View face‑coloured risk maps, click‑to‑highlight any issue, toggle wireframe, and draw clip‑plane regions. The built‑in viewer gives you full visual control.

📁

Export & Compliance

Generate pass/fail certificates, revision comparison reports, and a shareable summary snippet. All exports are standalone HTML – no viewer needed.
