FORGE doesn’t guess. It measures, computes, and tells you the truth.

Every recommendation is traced to an exact CAD face, a closed‑form physics formula, and the limits of your chosen manufacturing process.

Exact Geometry

FORGE reads your STEP file directly from the B‑Rep surfaces. No tessellation, no mesh, no approximation errors. Every measurement – wall thickness, pocket depth, corner radius – is taken from the exact CAD geometry you designed. What you see in the report is the real dimension, not a degraded copy.

Deterministic Physics

Structural reasoning is built on closed‑form engineering formulas. Cantilever deflection, von Mises stress, safety factor, buckling, natural frequency. Material properties – yield strength, Young’s modulus, density – are used directly. No AI, no machine learning, no statistical inference. Every number is auditable: you can trace it back to the formula and the input values.

Manufacturing Reality

FORGE applies the real limits of 3‑axis machining. It knows what a tool can reach, how deep a pocket can go with a given corner radius, and what wall thickness will deflect under your shop’s cutting force. When a geometry change is impractical, FORGE offers machining‑side alternatives: rest machining, long‑reach tools, reduced step‑down.
