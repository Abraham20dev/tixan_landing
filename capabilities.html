Capabilities

Every recommendation is traced to an exact CAD face, a closed‑form physics formula, and the limits of 3‑axis machining.

Exact Geometry

FORGE works directly on your STEP file’s B‑Rep surfaces. Every measurement—wall thickness, pocket depth, corner radius—is taken from the exact CAD geometry.

Deterministic Physics

Structural analysis uses closed‑form engineering formulas: cantilever deflection, von Mises stress, safety factor, buckling, natural frequency. Material properties—yield strength, Young’s modulus, density—are used directly.

Manufacturing Reality

FORGE applies the real limits of 3‑axis machining. It knows what a tool can reach, how deep a pocket can go with a given corner radius, and what wall thickness will deflect under your shop’s cutting force. When a geometry change is impractical, FORGE offers machining‑side alternatives: rest machining, long‑reach tools, reduced step‑down.
