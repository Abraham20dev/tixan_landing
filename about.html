Why TIXAN exists

Most manufacturing errors are discovered too late — after machining has started. That means wasted time, wasted material, and expensive redesign cycles.

TIXAN exists to move that decision earlier. It analyzes CAD geometry before production and tells you what will fail, so you can fix it while it is still just a model.

STEP + STL → Geometry analysis → Manufacturability decision

Principle

TIXAN is built on one idea:

If geometry is known, manufacturing risk should be predictable.
