Built for the Shop Floor

FORGE is a mesh‑free, offline desktop tool that answers “Can it withstand physical reality?” before a single chip is cut. It speaks three languages: manufacturability, structural integrity, and lifecycle – all from your exact CAD geometry.

The Three‑Domain Truth Layer

Manufacturability

Face‑level machining checks: thin walls, holes, deep pockets, tool access. Recommendations based on your chosen process limits.

Structural Integrity

Deterministic cantilever model. Deflection, safety factor, buckling. Exact required thickness to withstand your shop’s cutting force and material.

Lifecycle

Fatigue limit vs. applied stress. Know if your part will survive repeated use. Material‑aware, safety‑factor‑driven. Catch endurance risks before they become warranty claims.
