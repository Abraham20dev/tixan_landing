Why TIXAN exists

Manufacturing processes should be geometry plus physics-based reasoning. TIXAN approaches these manufacturability issue using First Principle Thinking. Our system questions about the fundamental truths and try to eliminate assumptions that contributes to poor decision making TIXAN is deterministic, offline desktop tool that reads your STEP and STL files, applies physics‑based heuristics, and gives you a face‑level manufacturability report – in seconds.

No cloud.Just semantic geometry you can verify.

What TIXAN gives you

📐

Face‑Level Reasoning

Every issue is traced to a specific CAD face with an exact measurement. No vague warnings.

🔬

Physics‑Based Heuristics

Recommendations come from first‑principle formulas – cantilever deflection, tool clearance, breakout risk – not statistical random data.

🧠

Shop‑Specific Learning

Mark issues as accepted and TIXAN remembers. Your shop’s intelligence stays local, making each report smarter.

🔐

100% Offline & Private

Your CAD data never leaves your machine. No internet required. Your intellectual property stays yours.

Built for engineers, by engineers

TIXAN was designed from the grounded engineering truth to be a key verifier before the CAD is brought to physical reality. a reliable, offline tool that answers “Can I make this part?” before a single chip is cut. It supports both STEP (CAD) and STL (mesh) files, so you can analyze models from any source.
